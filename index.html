About Jacob

For a long time, I always was somebody who would struggle with finding a career or field that I both was good at and enjoyed doing. After a few years of jumping between school and working, the company I was working with closing down due to the pandemic in early 2020.

After just getting any job I could during that time, I decided enough was enough and I wanted to develop new skills that would not only allow me to do something I enjoy, but also something I can continually learn and improve at. Now, here I am!

If you are looking to work with a developer that is great in a team setting or who is comfortable working on some solo projects, I'm your guy! I am looking forward to seeing what amazing projects we can work together on!
